WHERE LUXURY EMBRACES PARADISE

DAMAC Islands Phase 2 unveils a rare opportunity to own ultra-luxury beachfront townhouses in the heart of Dubailand. After the sell-out success of Phase 1, this exclusive release introduces limited 4- and 5-bedroom residences, starting from AED 2.6M, with handover scheduled for 2029. Designed with discerning families in mind, these homes combine contemporary architecture, open layouts, and premium finishes to deliver a lifestyle of sophistication and comfort.

Set against serene waterfront views and surrounded by lush green landscapes, the community is thoughtfully master-planned to inspire leisure, wellness, and togetherness. World-class amenities including infinity pools, fitness centers, landscaped parks, and vibrant retail promenades ensure that residents experience the perfect balance of relaxation, activity, and convenience—all within a secure and private environment.

With its prime beachfront location and excellent connectivity to Dubai’s major hubs, DAMAC Islands Phase 2 offers not just a luxurious lifestyle but also exceptional investment potential. Limited supply, growing demand for waterfront living, and the prestige of a DAMAC address make this community one of Dubailand’s most sought-after destinations—where exclusivity, luxury, and family living converge.
